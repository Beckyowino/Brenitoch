Admissions

Join Brenitoch School — where every child’s potential is nurtured, and every learner begins a journey toward excellence and character.

  1. Home
  2. Admissions

Enroll Early and Be Part of Our First Intake

Brenitoch School is now accepting applications for our inaugural academic year, beginning early next year. We welcome learners from Pre-Primary to Junior Secondary levels who are eager to learn, grow, and become responsible citizens in a caring and inspiring environment.

How to Apply

1

Pick an Application Form

Visit our school office or download the admission form from our website. Fill in your child’s details accurately and attach all required documents.

2

Submit Documents

Return the completed form with copies of your child’s birth certificate, recent report card (if applicable), and passport-sized photos to the admissions office or via email.

3

Attend an Interview or Assessment

Prospective students may be invited for an informal interview or short assessment to help us understand their academic level and interests.

4

Receive Admission Offer

Once your application is reviewed, we’ll contact you with the admission decision and next steps, including fee structure and orientation details.

Admission Requirements

Completed Application Form

Every applicant must submit a duly filled Brenitoch School admission form — available at the school or online.

Birth Certificate

A copy of the learner’s birth certificate for age verification is required during application.

Previous School Report (if applicable)

For students transferring from another school, please attach a recent academic report or transfer letter.

Passport Photos

Two recent passport-sized color photographs of the student should accompany the application form.
