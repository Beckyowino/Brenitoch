Preschool Program

Our Preschool program at Breinitoch Schools introduces young learners to a world of discovery through play-based learning. Designed for ages 3-5, the curriculum emphasizes creativity, social skills, and foundational literacy and numeracy, fostering a love for learning in a nurturing environment.

  • Focus on early literacy and numeracy
  • Creative arts and play-based activities
  • Safe and supportive classrooms

Elementary School Program

The Elementary School program at Breinitoch Schools (ages 5-11) builds a strong academic foundation through an engaging curriculum that integrates science, math, language arts, and social studies. Our hands-on approach encourages critical thinking and collaboration.

  • Core subjects with project-based learning
  • Emphasis on teamwork and communication
  • Extracurricular enrichment activities

Middle School Program

Breinitoch Schools’ Middle School program (ages 11-14) fosters independence and intellectual curiosity through interdisciplinary studies. Students explore advanced subjects, participate in leadership activities, and develop skills for academic and personal success.

  • Advanced coursework in STEM and humanities
  • Leadership and community service opportunities
  • Support for transitioning to high school

High School Program

Our High School program (ages 14-18) at Breinitoch Schools prepares students for higher education and global challenges with a rigorous college-preparatory curriculum, advanced placement courses, and opportunities for leadership and global engagement.

  • College-preparatory and AP courses
  • Career and college counseling
  • Global exchange and internship programs
