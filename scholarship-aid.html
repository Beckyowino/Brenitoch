Academic Excellence Scholarship

For Outstanding Academic Performers

Awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance in their previous school. This merit-based scholarship recognizes and rewards students who consistently achieve excellent grades and show strong potential for continued academic success.

Sibling Discount Program

10-20% Discount

For Families with Multiple Children

We value family partnerships and offer discounts when multiple children from the same family enroll at Brenitoch. The discount increases with the number of siblings enrolled, making quality education more affordable for larger families.

How to Apply for Financial Aid

  1. Complete the Application Form: Download and fill out our Financial Aid Application Form available on our website.
  2. Gather Required Documents: Collect supporting documents including income statements, student's academic records, and recommendation letters.
  3. Submit Your Application: Send your completed application and documents to our admissions office by the deadline.
  4. Interview Process: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with our financial aid committee.
  5. Notification: Successful applicants will receive notification within 2-3 weeks of the interview.
Application Deadline: Financial aid applications for the 2026 academic year close on December 15, 2025.
